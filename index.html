Thursday Meet

Every Thursday @ 9 P.M

This weekly Thursday Meet is a dedicated time of worship, prayer, and a one-hour Bible-based sermon held every Thursday at 9:00 PM. It is designed to bring believers together in unity to seek God’s presence, grow spiritually, and receive encouragement through the Word of God. The meeting includes worship songs, intercessory prayer, and a powerful message centered on the teachings of Jesus Christ.

ARK Meet 40 Days

40 Days Prayer 9 P.M

The ARK 40 Days Fasting Meet is a special spiritual season of prayer and fasting conducted daily for forty days, beginning in the last week of March and continuing until Easter. Each day at 9:00 PM, believers gather for worship, prayer, and a sermon focused on spiritual renewal, repentance, and deeper relationship with God. This program is a time of dedication, discipline, and revival in the presence of the Lord.

Words On Cross

Meditate 7 Words On Cross

The Words on the Cross Meet is a seven-day spiritual program held during Holy Week before Good Friday, focusing on the final seven statements of Jesus Christ on the cross. Each day at 9:00 PM, one word of Jesus is reflected upon through worship, prayer, and a one-hour message. This meeting helps believers understand the sacrifice of Christ and prepare their hearts for Good Friday and Easter through deep meditation and spiritual reflection.

Whatsapp Channel

Daily Verse @ 4 AM

The WhatsApp Channel is created for daily spiritual support, including Bible verses, prayer requests, and important live meeting updates. It also provides direct links for Google Meet sessions and special prayer gatherings that are not streamed on YouTube. This channel helps believers stay connected throughout the day with encouragement, scripture, and timely prayer guidance.
