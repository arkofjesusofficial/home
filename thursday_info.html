Thursday Prayer Meet

Ark Of JESUS Ministry

“PRAISE THE LORD 🙏”

The Thursday Prayer Meet is a weekly spiritual gathering conducted by Ark Of JESUS, where believers come together in unity to worship, pray, and receive the Word of God.

This meeting is held every Thursday at 9:00 PM. It is a dedicated time to seek God’s presence, grow spiritually, and strengthen faith through fellowship.

“Where two or three gather in My name, there am I with them.” – Matthew 18:20

📌 What Happens in the Prayer Meet?

Each session is structured to help believers experience the presence of God deeply:

🔹 Worship: The meeting begins with heartfelt worship, lifting the name of Jesus through songs and praise.

🔹 Prayer: A powerful time of prayer follows, where intercession, thanksgiving, and personal prayers are offered to God.

🔹 Word of God (Sermon): A spirit-filled message (approx. 1 hour) is shared from the Bible, bringing guidance, correction, encouragement, and truth.

🎯 Purpose of the Meeting

The Thursday Prayer Meet is conducted to:

✔ Strengthen faith in Jesus Christ
✔ Encourage believers in their spiritual journey
✔ Provide Biblical teaching and understanding
✔ Create a space for united prayer and worship

“JESUS IS THE WAY, THE TRUTH, AND THE LIFE”

⚠ Disclaimer

This prayer meeting and all related content are based on the teachings of the Holy Bible. The purpose of this gathering is purely spiritual and educational, focusing on Christian faith and the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Participation is voluntary, and viewers are encouraged to seek personal understanding and spiritual growth through prayer and Scripture.
