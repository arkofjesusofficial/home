7 Words on the Cross

Ark Of JESUS Special Holy Week Program
“7 Days • 7 Words • 1 Hour Prayer Meeting”

The 7 Words on the Cross Meet is a powerful spiritual program conducted by Ark Of JESUS during the final week before Good Friday.

This special event focuses on the **last seven words spoken by Jesus Christ on the Cross**, bringing deep reflection, worship, prayer, and spiritual understanding.

The program runs for 7 consecutive days, each day focusing on one word from the Cross of Calvary.

Each session is held for 1 hour daily, starting at 9:00 PM, leading believers into prayer, worship, and message-based reflection.

Program Details

Duration

7 Days

Start Time

9:00 PM Daily

Focus

7 Words of Jesus

Season

Before Good Friday

The 7 Words

Day 1

Father, forgive them

Day 2

Today you will be with Me in Paradise

Day 3

Woman, behold your son

Day 4

My God, My God, why have You forsaken Me?

Day 5

I thirst

Day 6

It is finished

Day 7

Father, into Your hands I commit My spirit

This meeting is a time of deep spiritual reflection on the sacrifice of Jesus Christ. Believers are encouraged to participate daily in prayer, worship, and fasting.

The focus is to prepare hearts before Good Friday and Easter through repentance and faith.
