40 Days Prayer Meet

Ark Of JESUS Special Season of Prayer & Fasting
“40 Days of Prayer, Worship & Spiritual Growth”

The 40 Days Prayer Meet is a special spiritual season held under Ark Of JESUS, dedicated to prayer, worship, fasting, and Bible meditation. This powerful meeting is conducted every year as a time of deep spiritual renewal.

It begins in the last week of March and continues daily until just before Easter. During this period, believers come together in unity for 40 continuous days of prayer.

Each session includes 1 hour of spiritual gathering consisting of worship, prayer, Bible-based sermon, and prophetic encouragement.

The meeting starts daily at 9:00 PM, allowing believers from different places to join and participate in the presence of God.

Participants are encouraged to practice fasting according to their personal strength and spiritual guidance. The focus is not only on physical fasting but also on spiritual cleansing, repentance, and drawing closer to God.

Every day is a step of faith, healing, and transformation through the presence of Jesus Christ.
